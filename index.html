Sankalp: Government Scheme Aid Insight Portal

Sankalp Insight Portal is a data-driven web application designed to streamline the evaluation of government scheme requests using intelligent machine learning models. Built with a focus on accuracy and transparency, the platform allows users to upload request data, visualize trends, and predict approval status, sanctioned amounts, and estimated beneficiaries in real-time. The system utilizes a classification model to assess the likelihood of approval and regression models for financial and impact estimations, providing decision-makers and analysts with actionable insights. With an interactive dashboard, clean visualizations, and a user-friendly interface, the portal bridges the gap between complex data and meaningful understanding. Developed by a dedicated team of students, this project showcases the integration of AI and data science into public welfare, helping ensure more informed and equitable scheme distribution. The portal reflects our vision of combining technology and social impact for the greater good.
